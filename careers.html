Role Descriptions

Seedify


Seedify is looking for applicants who are experienced in being a shopkeeper, social media manager and accountant. See the requirements below if you are interested and click the apply button.

Shopkeeper

You are a plant-impassioned and team-oriented individual who has a balance of retail, customer service experience and plant knowledge. You love to meet like-minded plant enthusiasts as well as patiently teaching plant novices about all the amazing things Seedify has to offer. You must have a love for fast paced, startup environments, are highly self-motivated and have the ability to maintain a positive, resilient attitude in the face of growth and change. You will be expected to work 15-28 hours/week depending on availability and to assist with Seedify's In-Store evening workshops on a weekly basis.

Social Media Manager

This role requires a social media expert, skilled at concepting social content that both defines the brand’s tone of voice and results in high-value audience growth and business impact. As a result, your approach to social media must be community-oriented, focused on creating a two-way dialogue that inspires customer advocacy and in turn, drives conversion. If you’re customer-led, brand-obsessed and fascinated by lifestyle and retail trends, we look forward to hearing from you.This role reports to the Director of Brand Marketing and manages our Social Media & Production Coordinator.

Accountant

The Accountant will collaborate with the Finance team (which consists of our Chief Financial Officer and Financial Analyst) and Graphite (our outsourced accounting firm) to strengthen The Sill’s accounting practices.
In this role, you will work in tandem with the CFO to enhance our reporting processes (including credit, A/R, A/P, balance sheets, reconciliations, month-end, and quarter-end). You will drive success for the business by developing financial controls and process improvement initiatives, advancing our ability to forecast and plan for our long-term goals.
