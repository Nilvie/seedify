Frequently Asked Questions

My order arrived damage, what do i do?

Every plant is fragile and the shipping process is not always kind (or easy). Most of the time we get it right, but sometimes we don’t. When that happens, we fix it – be it a damaged plant or pot due to shipping carrier mishandling. Please inspect your package when you receive your delivery. Should your order arrive damaged, please fill out our contact form (make sure to include a photo) and we’ll get back to you in two business days. The form ensures we have what’s needed to help you as soon as possible! In the unlikely event a plant is received dead on arrival, it will always be replaced.

How will my plant arrive?

Our number one priority is that our plants arrive in happy, healthy condition. Some of our plants ship directly potted in our earthenware plants, while others ship in their nursery grow pot placed inside the planter.

Do i have to repot my plant once it arrives?

The short answer is, no. First, let your plant acclimate to its new environment. Place the plant in the spot you plan to keep it in. Let it settle in for about two weeks.

How does deliver work?

We deliver seven days a week, and your delivery should be with you 6-12 days after placing your order.

Within Philippines, we deliver between the following hours:

Monday-Wednesday - 2pm-10pm
Thursday-Sunday - 9am-4pm

We’ll text and email you the day before your delivery to give you a more precise time slot.

For deliveries outside Philippines, you’ll receive an email with a link to your order status page once your order is out for delivery.

If you’re not at home when delivery is attempted, our drivers will usually leave your order in your designated safe place (we’ll ask you for this at check out). If that’s not possible, delivery will be attempted up to two more times.

Do you have No Contact Delivery due to Covid 19 ?

We’re still delivering as normal, but we’re switching to zero-contact delivery until further notice.
When your driver delivers your order, they'll the place items at the door, ring the bell using a glove and step back. They will then allow you to the collect your items before retrieving any delivery boxes (if you are in our Philippines delivery area).

If you’d prefer to have your order just left in your safe place, please let us know at checkout.
